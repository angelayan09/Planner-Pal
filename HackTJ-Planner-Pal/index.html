Planner Pal

Planner Pal helps you organize your study schedule based on the tasks you need to complete!
  • Start by entering the time period you have to work, your tasks, their relative rank of difficulty (1 to 6, with 1 being the hardest; two tasks cannot have the same rank), and how long you think you'll need to complete them.
  • Then click the "Get your schedule" button, and Planner Pal will display your recommended study schedule!
